AEROFARMS: IDENTITY

The purpose of this project was to design a typographic booklet and cover for the first two chapters of A Tale of Two Cities: A Story of the French Revolution, by Charles Dickens. Contraints included using type only, black and white only for color, and a single body of text on the interior pages. I am using Brown for display text and Sentinel for body copy.
