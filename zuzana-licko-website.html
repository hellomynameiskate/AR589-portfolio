ZUZANA LICKO WEBSITE

The purpose of this project was to design a website showcasing the work of female typographer Zuzana Licko. Licko developed her first typefaces on the computer in its early days, resulting in a bitmapped aesthetic. I chose to break apart the lettering when featuring Licko's Lo-Res typeface to emphasize the pixelated makeup of her design.
