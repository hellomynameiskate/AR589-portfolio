FINANCE IS FEMALE

How is finance different for women? For one, we live longer than men. We make less money than men. And we take more breaks during our careers. (This is all generally speaking, of course.)

So, shouldn't our our financial education and goals reflect these diferences? Absolutely.

For my MFA thesis, I designed and branded finance is female, an app meant to close the gender gap in all things money management by educating young women about saving, investing, and more. The double "F" logo mark resembles a hashtag (or number sign).
